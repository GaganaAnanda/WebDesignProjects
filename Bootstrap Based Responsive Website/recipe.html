Ingredients

For Dosa:

Ingredient Quantity
Rice 2 cups
Urad dal 1 cup
Fenugreek Seeds 1 tbsp
Salt To taste
Oil As needed

For Chicken Sukka:

Ingredient Quantity
Raw Chicken 500 grams
Onion 1 medium
Coconut 1/2 cup
Curry Leaves 1 sprig
Spices To taste

Cooking Instructions

1

Prepare Dosa Batter

Soak rice and urad dal for 4-6 hours. Grind to smooth batter. Add salt and ferment overnight.

2

Make Chicken Sukka

Marinate chicken with spices. Cook with onions and coconut until dry and crispy.

3

Cook the Dosa

Heat griddle, spread batter thinly. Drizzle oil. Cook until golden and crispy.

4

Serve Hot

Serve crispy dosa with chicken sukka, coconut chutney, and sambar.

Chef's Tips üí°

Pro Tips
  • Ferment batter in warm place for 8-12 hours
  • Use cast iron griddle for crispiest dosa
  • Add curry leaves for authentic flavor
  • Keep flame medium for even cooking
